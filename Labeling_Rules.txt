Labeling Rules (Draft)
Missing Bond
- Pad is empty
- No wire present
- No bond foot visible
Lifted Bond Foot
- Wire is present
- Foot is detached or partially lifted
- Pad contact is broken
Disfigured Bond
- Wire is present
- Bond is malformed (bent, twisted, collapsed, wrong loop height)
- Foot is attached
Debris
- Any foreign object
- Dust, particles, solder balls, contamination
Tape in Stepped Hole
- Any tape visible inside or covering the alignment hole
None
- No defects present
- All bonds clean and properly formed
